Product Overview

Ideagen Academy is the cloud-based e-learning solution that allows your entire workforce to become fully competent using Ideagen’s software suite quickly, easily and on their own terms.

Our state-of-the-art training software provides a stimulating variety of interactive demos, activities and assessments that encourages your employees to become self-sufficient learners, saving your organisation a wealth of valuable time and money.

Academy is designed to prioritise learning and development in a way that suits you, so that your organisation can achieve the maximum return on investment in Ideagen software. It also delivers complete control and visibility of all training activities, ensuring you can monitor and measure progress in real time.

Who Is Academy For?

Learners

Ideagen Academy teaches users of Q-Pulse, Coruson and Pentana key skills that will enable them to improve their productivity and performance. It is ideal for new learning and refresher courses, and is available 24/7 on desktop, laptop or tablet devices.

Instructors

Ensure the competency of your employees with Academy’s complete training curricula.

Instructors can add their own courses, create renewable certificates, and monitor training with sleek, intuitive reporting dashboards.

Administrators

Enjoy complete clarity and control over users’ progress and development. In addition to the permissions given to instructors, administrators can also create groups and sub-branches, and add or import new users.
